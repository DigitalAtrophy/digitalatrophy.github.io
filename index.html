Web Security and You!

  • Keep Strong Passwords

    • Quite often, our passwords are not as strong as we think they are, and many of the password restrictions sites dictate are detrimental.

  • Keep up to Date

    • Making sure your software is up to date is very important, as updates frequently come with security fixes.

  • Keep It to Yourself

    • Social media can be a great tool to interact with people you know, but be wary of what and how much information you share online, as well as who you are sharing it with.
